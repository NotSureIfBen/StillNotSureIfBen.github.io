CinemaScope

About

CinemaScope originally started as an event my brother'd host for friends and family. It was an outdoor cinema, which would play whatever film recently came out on blu-ray. We'd have a sheet of white cloth which would have a film projected onto it, a PS3 to play the film and a projector to put the film onto the "screen". This was a yearly thing which then evolved into what CinemaScope is today; A fully-fledged business with hundreds of supporting patrons.

We're making sure that nothing we do will lessen a person's experience here at CinemaScope. If you have any complaints, please log one via the contact section. The more criticism we get, the more we can improve our services.

Please bear in mind that we're a small number of staff so in comparison to other cinemas, we may be slightly slower. Thank you for your continued support and help.
